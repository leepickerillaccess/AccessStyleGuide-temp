Buttons

As a general rule of thumb; choose one colour for positive actions, no more than three neutral colours and one negative (only if necessary). The positive colour can be any from the primary or secondary pallete, depending on the product branding.

Positive

These can be used with any Primary colour, simply add the class colour to .btn- e.g .btn-atoll
